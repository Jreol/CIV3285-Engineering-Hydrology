CIV3285 - Engineering Hydrology

Introduction

Unit Objectives

This unit addresses the common issues of Hydrology with a particular focus on Urban Water Management (UWM)

There are three components in the unit:

  1. General Introduction to hydrology
  2. Hydrology in an urban contenxt
  3. Water Quality

What does the Unit aims to give

  • Quantify the hydrological events by understanding the processes of the water cycle;
  • understand the characteristics of major types of urban stormwater;
  • understand the functioning of major systems aused in urban water management (UWM)
  • conceptually design UWM systems (e.g. drainage systems);
  • identify sources of water quality issues
  • critically assess any information provided;
  • make decisions based on your knowledge and identify and utilise additional sources outside of those provided;
  • work in teams and defend design choices;
  • creatively develop design alternatives and elaborate on choices made.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Undertake hydrological investigations of natural and urban catchments
  2. estimate floods for engineering design and planning
  3. conceptually design and estimate flows of a minor drainage network
  4. design gutters, swales, and pits
  5. produce technical reports developed by teams at the standard required by the engineering professions

This unit will help you learn the fundamental skills essential for carrying out hydrology-related projects.

Real-life projects will be much more complex.

Topic 1 - Principles of Hydrology

9 Lectures, 1 Assignment:

  • main characteristics of the global water cycle;
  • identification of the main hydrological processes;
  • determination of statistically likely events;
  • quantification of design storms and floods;
  • simple modelling of the water cycle;

Topic 2 - Urban Stormwater Management

9+1 Lectures, 1 Assignment

  • main principles of stormwater management;
  • rainfall and flood estimation;
  • impacts of urbanisation and introduction to Water Sensitive Urban Desing (WSUD);
  • minor and major design of urban drainage

Topic 3 - Water Quality

2 Lectures

  • Introduction to Water Treatment Systems;
  • Types of waterways contamination;
  • sources detection and impact modelling;
